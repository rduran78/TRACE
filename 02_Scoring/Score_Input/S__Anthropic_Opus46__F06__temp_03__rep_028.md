### Bottleneck A: `build_neighbor_lookup` ├втВмтАЭ O(n) list of per-row lookups via `lapply`

### Why it takes 86+ hours

- ~6.46M iterations ├ГтАФ 5 variables ├ГтАФ expensive per-element R-level operations = billions of interpreted R operations.
